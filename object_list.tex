\section*{Overview}
The IWY Assistant utilizes the \textbf{COCO-SSD (Common Objects in Context - Single Shot Detector)} lightweight model. This model is specifically optimized for mobile execution and can recognize \textbf{80 distinct categories} of objects.

\section*{Note on Specific Hazards}
While "stairs" are a priority in our code's safety logic, the base COCO-SSD model often classifies them under broad spatial categories or requires a custom-trained layer for higher accuracy. The current implementation prioritizes \textbf{Vehicles, People, and Signage} as primary navigational hazards.
